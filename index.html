Personal Information

Dr. Chensheng Liu is now a distinguished research fellow at Department of Automation, and the Key Laboratory of Smart Manufacturing in Energy Chemical Process, Ministry of Education, East China University of Science & Technology, Shanghai, China, where he was a postdoctoral fellow from 2019 to 2021. From 2018 to 2019, he conducted his postdoctoral research with University of Alberta, Edmonton, AB, Canada.

    • Research Interests

    My research interests lie in the areas of cyber-physical system and smart grid. Currently, I am working on the following research topics:
  • Machine learning in smart grid
  • Security of cyber-physical systems
  • Smart grid optimization and control
